HivePoint

You finally get a prospect in front of your website, sales presentation, product, or product demo. You have 90 seconds to wow them. Static text, images, and even videos no longer cut it. We believe that interactivity engages.

What is HivePoint?

We are a small team that is passionate about building fully interactive user interfaces. We're experts in rapid development of engaging, interactive, fully operational end-to-end user-facing systems using the latest web technologies.

Show me an example

We are the people behind Braid magazine which provides us with a way to use interactivity to tell interesting stories in a new way.

Communicating with people is about telling stories. In the past, that meant writing. For example, when 9 prisoners were recently released from the Guantanamo Bay prison, the Guardian wrote this 1000 word story about it.

We told the story of the Guantanamo Bay detainees by using interactive graphics to let readers visualize and explore the data.

How we work

Your website, product, or sales presentation tells a story. We work on contract to help make it more engaging through interactivity.

We prefer to start small. You identify part of an existing user-facing system and we'll offer a proposal on how to increase its interactivity.

We then rapidly prototype a solution and deliver it (typically in days or weeks) in the way that best fits the situation. In many cases, what we deliver can be dropped into an existing resource (like a website or presentation) with minimum disruption.
